Welcome to the Rainbow Webpage!

This is a colorful webpage that celebrates the beauty of rainbows.

What is a Rainbow?

A rainbow is a meteorological phenomenon that is caused by reflection, refraction and dispersion of light in water droplets resulting in a spectrum of light appearing in the sky.

Colors of the Rainbow

The colors of the rainbow, in order, are red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet.

Red

Red is the color of fire and blood. It is associated with energy, war, danger, strength, power, and love.

Orange

Orange is the color of sunshine, warmth, and happiness. It is associated with enthusiasm, creativity, success, and stimulation.

Yellow

Yellow is the color of sunshine, hope, and happiness. It is associated with joy, intellect, and energy.

Green

Green is the color of nature and the environment. It is associated with growth, harmony, freshness, and fertility.

Blue

Blue is the color of the sky and the sea. It is associated with calmness, stability, trust, and loyalty.

Indigo

Indigo is a deep blue color that is associated with intuition, perception, and spirituality.

Violet

Violet is a color of the rainbow that is associated with creativity, spirituality, and royalty.
